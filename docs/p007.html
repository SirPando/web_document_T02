Snake Plant

Category: Plant

Snake Plant is a tree with strong vitality, but you should not forget them because of that. Put the plant in enough light with a little shade and it's great. And you should be extremely careful when watering, try to keep the soil not too wet, too much water can damage the roots of the plant, so I only need to water just enough on the surface of the soil, usually 2-3. I water it once a week.
